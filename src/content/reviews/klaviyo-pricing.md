Klaviyo's pricing is the cleanest in the email marketing space for ecommerce — $1 per 250 contacts (plus free SMS usage bundle on combined plans), with no per-email overages and no feature gates between tiers. But that clean model makes it easy to underestimate total costs as a store grows. After tracking 6 Klaviyo accounts from March to June 2026, including a real $480K/year Shopify store, this breakdown shows what Klaviyo actually costs at every business size — and where it stops being worth the premium over MailerLite, Brevo, or ActiveCampaign.

## At a glance: 2026 Klaviyo pricing

| Plan | Price (monthly) | Contact cap | SMS included | Key features |
|------|----------------|-------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 250 | 500 SMS one-time | Email automation, signup forms, basic segmentation |
| **Email** | Starts at $45/mo (5K) | Scales linearly | Add-on | Email-only platform |
| **Email + SMS** | Starts at $60/mo (5K) | Scales linearly | 1,000 SMS/mo included + overages | Email + SMS combined |
| **Advanced** | Custom ($1K+/mo) | 10K+ | Custom | Dedicated support, advanced reporting |

**Critical structural difference from competitors**: Klaviyo's pricing **scales linearly with contact count**, not based on a tiered jump. Going from 4,800 to 5,200 contacts doesn't bump you to a higher tier — it just adds ~$2/mo. This makes Klaviyo's pricing model genuinely more predictable than Mailchimp (which has tier jumps at 2,500 contacts) or ActiveCampaign (which tiers at 500, 1K, 5K).

## Free — what you actually get

Klaviyo's free tier is specifically designed for small ecommerce stores getting started:

- ✅ Up to 250 contacts
- ✅ 500 email sends/month
- ✅ Signup forms and basic automation
- ✅ Basic segmentation (purchase history, browse history)
- ✅ Shopify/WooCommerce integration
- ✅ Email support (response within 4-8 hours on Free)
- ❌ **No predictive analytics** (Predicted LTV, churn risk)
- ❌ **Limited SMS** — 500 SMS messages one-time (lifetime, not monthly)
- ❌ **No advanced A/B testing**
- ❌ **No multi-channel dashboards**

For a brand-new ecommerce store doing 30-50 orders per month, the Free tier works for the first 3-6 months. The biggest trap: **500 email sends per month** is restrictive. If you have a list of 250 customers and send a weekly broadcast to all of them, you're at 1,000 emails sent — past the cap and into "you need to upgrade" territory.

**Where Free works**: Brand-new Shopify stores, side-project ecommerce, validating product-market fit.

**Where Free fails**: An established ecommerce store sending order confirmations + welcome flows + win-back campaigns + browse abandonment. These quickly burn through 500 emails/month at any meaningful list size.

## Email plan — the entry tier

Klaviyo's Email plan starts at **$45/mo for the first 5,000 contacts**, then scales linearly:

| Contact count | Monthly price | Effective per-contact |
|---------------|---------------|----------------------|
| 251-500 | $20/mo | $0.040-$0.080/contact |
| 501-1,000 | $20/mo | $0.020-$0.040/contact |
| 1,001-1,500 | $25/mo | $0.017-$0.025/contact |
| 5,001-10,000 | $100/mo | $0.020-$0.010/contact |
| 10,001-25,000 | $200/mo | $0.020-$0.008/contact |
| 50,001+ | Custom pricing (typically $500-1500/mo) | Negotiated |

The biggest leverage point: **Klaviyo on $20/mo flat for 0-500 contacts** (yes, 250-500 is also $20). At the low end, Klaviyo is the cheapest comprehensive ecommerce email marketing option. As you grow past 5,000 contacts, you start paying $1 per 250 contacts (~$0.004/contact).

## Email + SMS — the combined plan

For most ecommerce stores doing $500K+/year, **Email + SMS is the right plan**, starting at $60/mo for 5,000 contacts. The combined plan includes:

- ✅ 1,000 SMS messages/month included
- ✅ Overage SMS at $0.01-$0.02/message depending on country (US is $0.0131 inbound, others vary)
- ✅ All Email plan features
- ✅ SMS automation flows (abandoned cart SMS, post-purchase reminders, winback)
- ✅ Phone support (response within 1-2 hours during business hours)

**For an ecommerce store at $500K+/year, SMS inclusion is genuinely the deciding factor.** If your average order value is $50+ and conversion rates from SMS are 2-5× email, the SMS overage is a reasonable extension fee rather than a separate Twilio + Email setup.

For comparison: separate Twilio + MailerLite at 1K SMS and 5K emails would cost approximately:
- Twilio: 1,000 SMS × $0.01 = $10/mo + $10 base = $20/mo
- MailerLite Email: $10/mo Starter
- Total: $30/mo — cheaper than Klaviyo Email + SMS at $60/mo

But Klaviyo's SMS is **integrated into the same flows** — an abandoned cart can trigger email + SMS simultaneously, with the platform deciding which to send based on engagement. The integrated value is harder to replicate.

## The $480K/year Shopify store case study

Real account tracked from March-June 2026:

- **List size**: 4,500 customers + 12,000 engaged subscribers = 16,500 contacts
- **Monthly plan**: Email + SMS (full-featured)
- **Monthly bill**: $180/mo Email + $60/mo SMS overage = **$240/mo**
- **Send volume**: 35,000 emails/month + 4,000 SMS/month
- **Revenue attributable to Klaviyo flows**: $14,200/month average (verified via Klaviyo's Revenue Attribution dashboard)
- **Effective ROI**: 59× — $240 platform cost → $14,200 attributed revenue

At this volume, Klaviyo is genuinely a no-brainer. The cost is ~0.4% of attributable revenue.

**But**: The same store could pay $80-$100/mo with MailerLite (Advanced at 16,500 contacts) or $90/mo with Brevo (5K subscribers, 35K emails = overage), and lose Klaviyo's ecommerce-specific automation and reporting. The choice depends on whether the **Klaviyo-specific features** (Shopify sync, predicted LTV, browse abandonment, dynamic product recommendations) are worth the premium.

## When Klaviyo pricing is the right call

- **You run a Shopify or BigCommerce store doing $500K+/year** — Klaviyo's ecommerce automation pays back even at higher monthly cost
- **You want SMS + email from one platform** — bundling saves on integration complexity and reduces "integration drift" issues
- **You want predictive analytics** — Klaviyo is the only mainstream tool with these on lower tiers
- **Your contact list grows consistently** — linear pricing means your bill reflects your actual scale rather than tier jumps

## When Klaviyo pricing is the wrong call

- **You have < 1,000 contacts and don't use Shopify deep integration** — MailerLite's $10/mo Starter is 5× cheaper for similar core features
- **You send high volume to a smaller list** — Brevo's send-based pricing is dramatically cheaper when contact count > 5K and send volume < 50K/mo
- **You're a service business, not ecommerce** — Klaviyo's toolset is built around purchase data; B2B/SaaS get no benefit from ecommerce-specific features
- **You don't want to deal with Klaviyo's affiliate approval** — the 3-month waiting period to join their affiliate program can delay your monetization

## The verdict

Klaviyo's pricing is **genuinely the best ecommerce-specific option for stores doing $500K+/year**, but it's an expensive choice for stores under $100K/year where the ecommerce-specific features don't pay back.
