Sendlane is the email marketing platform that quietly powers thousands of e-commerce stores. Founded in 2013, it was one of the first platforms to focus exclusively on e-commerce email and SMS marketing, before "omnichannel" became the industry standard.

The platform's key differentiator is **behavior-based automations** — automations that trigger on real customer actions like product page views, cart abandonment, browsing patterns, and purchase history. Combined with email, SMS, and push notifications in one platform, Sendlane is a serious alternative to Klaviyo for e-commerce stores that want unified customer journey orchestration.

We tested Sendlane for 90 days across three online stores: a 1,500-contact Shopify apparel store, a 5,000-contact WooCommerce supplement brand, and a 12,000-contact BigCommerce electronics retailer. Here's the honest verdict.

## Who Sendlane is for

Sendlane is built for:

- **E-commerce stores with 500-25,000 contacts** that need sophisticated automations
- **Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce stores** that want deep platform integrations
- **Marketers who want email + SMS + push in one platform** — Sendlane unifies all three channels
- **Stores that value behavior-based automation** — Sendlane tracks browse behavior, cart activity, and purchase patterns

Sendlane is **NOT** for:

- **Anyone not running an online store** — Sendlane's e-commerce focus is wasted on non-retail use cases

## The behavior-based automation advantage

Sendlane's **behavior-based automations** are the platform's strongest feature. Here's how they differ from competitors:

**Standard automations (Mailchimp, MailerLite, ActiveCampaign):**
- Triggered by email events (open, click)
- Triggered by signup date or tag
- Triggered by webhook or date

**Sendlane's behavior automations:**
- **Browse abandonment** — trigger when a contact views a product but doesn't add to cart
- **Cart abandonment** — trigger with product-specific recommendations
- **Browse-to-buy** — trigger when a contact browses and then purchases (capture intent)
- **Post-purchase browse** — trigger when a contact views related products after purchase
- **Win-back** — trigger based on inactivity patterns
- **VIP customer identification** — automatically tag high-value customers based on purchase patterns

**Compared to Klaviyo:** Both platforms support behavior-based automations. Klaviyo's are slightly more sophisticated (Klaviyo has predictive analytics on top). Sendlane's are easier to set up and use.

**Compared to Mailchimp:** Mailchimp supports browse abandonment, but it's paywalled above the Essentials tier. Sendlane's behavior automations are included on the Pro plan.

**For e-commerce stores running browse abandonment, cart abandonment, and post-purchase sequences:** Sendlane's automation builder is a strong choice.

### Does Sendlane have a free tier?

No — Sendlane's minimum is $29/mo for 500 contacts. Klaviyo offers a free tier up to 250 contacts. For a permanent free tier, Mailchimp or MailerLite is better.

### Can Sendlane replace my SMS platform?

Yes — Sendlane's built-in SMS supports US, Canada, UK, and Australia. Most e-commerce stores can consolidate SMS into Sendlane and save on separate SMS platform costs.
