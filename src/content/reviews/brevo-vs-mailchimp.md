Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) and Mailchimp have been competing for years, but the 2026 pricing models are more different than ever. After tracking 6 Brevo accounts and 12 Mailchimp accounts from March to June 2026, this comparison shows where each tool genuinely wins.

## Pricing comparison: Brevo vs Mailchimp

For a small business sending marketing newsletters + occasional transactional email:

| Tool | 1K contacts, marketing + transactional |
|------|------------------------------------------|
| **Brevo Lite** | $9/mo + transactional included (overages for high volume) |
| **Mailchimp Essentials + Mandrill** | $13/mo + $20/mo Mandrill = $33/mo |

**Brevo is 3.5x cheaper at this tier**. The $9/mo includes transactional email bundled.

For higher volume senders:
| Tool | 5K contacts |
|------|-------------|
| **Brevo** | $9-50/mo depending on email volume |
| **Mailchimp** | $135+/mo Standard |

Brevo's pricing is genuinely the cheapest in the category at the small-business tier.

## When Brevo wins over Mailchimp

- **You need transactional email bundled** with marketing email — Brevo's $9/mo bundle is genuinely the cheapest single-vendor option
- **You have <1,000 contacts and want predictable monthly pricing** — Brevo's $9/mo Lite vs Mailchimp's $13/mo Essentials
- **You send high volumes to a large list** — Brevo's send-based pricing wins on price
- **You want EU jurisdiction** — Brevo is French (now EU-domiciled after 2023 restructure), GDPR-compliant by default

## When Mailchimp wins over Brevo

- **You run multi-channel campaigns** (email + SMS + landing pages + social) — Mailchimp's broader feature ecosystem is more mature
- **You need sophisticated reporting** (custom attribution, revenue tracking)
- **You use Mailchimp's social posting or ad sync features**
- **You have existing Mailchimp templates / automations** you don't want to migrate

## The verdict

For most small businesses in 2026, **Brevo is the better pick** based on the bundled transactional + marketing pricing. Mailchimp's edge is multi-channel features — if you genuinely use them, Mailchimp wins; if your workflow is email-focused, Brevo saves 50-70% per month.
