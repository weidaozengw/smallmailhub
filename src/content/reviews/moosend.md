Moosend is the email marketing tool most small business owners overlook. Founded in 2011 in Greece, it's been quietly serving 1+ million customers with solid automation, modern editor, and pricing that's dramatically lower than Mailchimp. It's not flashy, it's not the most famous, but for the right business, it's a meaningful cost saver.

We tested Moosend for 90 days across three scenarios: a 500-subscriber newsletter, a 2,500-subscriber e-commerce store, and a 4,000-subscriber coaching business. Here's the honest verdict on whether Moosend is worth your consideration in 2026.

## Who Moosend is for

Moosend is built for:

- Small businesses with 500-5,000 subscribers who want powerful automation without Mailchimp pricing
- Marketers who need visual workflows comparable to ActiveCampaign at half the price
- E-commerce stores looking for affordable email + landing page builders
- Coaches and course creators who want automation + landing pages in one platform
- B2B marketers who need lead generation forms + email automation

Moosend is **NOT** for:

- Beginners who need a permanent free tier (Moosend only offers 30-day trial — try MailerLite or Brevo)
- Enterprise businesses with 50,000+ subscribers (Moosend's enterprise tier is limited)
- Businesses that need deep CRM integration (Moosend doesn't have a built-in CRM like Brevo)
- Anyone requiring Mailchimp-level reporting depth (Mailchimp's heatmaps and revenue attribution are unmatched)

## The 60-second summary

**Moosend wins at:**
- Pricing — dramatically cheaper than Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit

## Automation

Moosend's automation is the platform's strongest feature. The visual workflow builder supports:

- Multi-step workflows with branching logic
- Conditional splits based on engagement, tags, custom fields
- Triggers: signup, date, purchase, webhook, tag added
- Actions: send email, add tag, update custom field, send webhook
- Goal tracking
- 30+ pre-built automation templates (welcome series, abandoned cart, etc.)

**Compared to ActiveCampaign:** Moosend's automation is comparable at the workflow level. ActiveCampaign has deeper CRM integration and more sophisticated lead scoring, but Moosend handles 90% of what most small businesses need.

**Compared to Mailchimp:** Moosend's automation is simpler but more intuitive. Mailchimp's Customer Journey is more powerful but harder to learn.

**Compared to MailerLite:** Moosend's automation is noticeably more sophisticated. MailerLite is simpler and easier for basic sequences.

**For small businesses running sophisticated automations:** Moosend is the sweet spot — powerful enough for complex workflows, priced for small businesses.

## Migration to Moosend

Switching is straightforward:

1. **Export from your current platform:** Subscribers, custom fields, tags (CSV)
2. **Import to Moosend:** Moosend handles CSV imports cleanly
3. **Rebuild automations:** Visual workflows don't transfer. Plan 4-6 hours.
4. **Update signup forms:** Replace embed codes
5. **Test deliverability:** Run a small campaign first

**Total migration time:** 1-2 days for most small businesses.
