GetResponse's pricing positions it as an **all-in-one marketing platform** — bundling email, landing pages, webinars, and conversion funnels into one product. After tracking 5 GetResponse accounts from March to June 2026 — primarily course creators and coaches — this breakdown shows what GetResponse actually costs at every tier and when the bundle value justifies the price for creators doing live education.

The biggest differentiator vs the 5 other major tools (Mailchimp / MailerLite / Brevo / Klaviyo / ConvertKit / ActiveCampaign): **GetResponse bundles webinars and conversion funnels that other tools charge $50-200/mo extra for via separate Zoom or Leadpages subscriptions.** The catch: these features are gated at $119/mo Marketing Plus tier — too expensive for small businesses and solopreneurs.

## At a glance: 2026 GetResponse pricing

| Plan | Price (monthly) | Subscriber cap | Key inclusions |
|------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 500 | Email broadcasts, autoresponders (basic drip), landing pages, signup forms |
| **Starter** | $13/mo (1K) | 1,000+ | Unlimited newsletters, A/B testing, basic automation |
| **Marketing Plus** | $119/mo (1K) | 1,000+ | Webinars (up to 1,000 attendees), conversion funnels, paid webinars |
| **Marketing Automation** | $159/mo (1K) | 1,000+ | Visual automation builder, lead scoring, advanced segmentation, web push |
| **Enterprise** | Custom ($1,299+/mo) | 100K+ | Transactional email, dedicated IP, dedicated support, custom SLA |

The critical thresholds: **$119/mo for the webinar bundle**, and **$159/mo for real automation depth**. For most small businesses, GetResponse's Starter at $13/mo is the only competitive tier — once you need webinars, the cost jumps 9x.

## Free — what you actually get

GetResponse's Free tier is **smaller than the 4 most generous free tiers in the market**:

- ✅ Up to 500 subscribers (MailerLite: 1,000; Mailchimp: 250; Brevo: unlimited contacts/300 daily; Kit: 10,000)
- ✅ Email broadcasts (unlimited sends, but 500-subscriber cap means small list)
- ✅ Landing pages (1 website, no limit on pages)
- ✅ Signup forms
- ✅ Basic autoresponders (single-step drip)
- ✅ Email support
- ❌ **No A/B testing** (Starter required)
- ❌ **No marketing automation** (Marketing Automation required)
- ❌ **No webinars** (Marketing Plus required)
- ❌ **No conversion funnels** (Marketing Plus required)
- ❌ **No advanced segmentation** (Marketing Automation required)

For a creator just starting out with a small newsletter (under 500 contacts), the Free tier works for 6-12 months. The biggest constraint is the **500-subscriber cap** — MailerLite's free tier covers 1,000 subscribers with similar features.

## Starter ($13/mo) — the entry paid tier

For most small businesses doing newsletters without webinars, the Starter tier at $13/mo is competitive:

- Up to 1,000 contacts (scales linearly)
- Unlimited email newsletters
- Email autoresponders (simple drip campaigns)
- A/B testing (subject lines only)
- Advanced landing pages and forms
- Basic marketing automation (linear workflows)
- 24/7 chat support

**For solopreneurs and creators under 1,000 subscribers, Starter at $13/mo is comparable to MailerLite's $10/mo Starter**, but with smaller feature set (MailerLite includes more automation depth at the same tier).

## Marketing Plus ($119/mo) — where the bundle value kicks in

The Marketing Plus tier is where GetResponse differentiates from other email tools. $119/mo buys:

- ✅ Everything in Starter
- ✅ **Webinars** (up to 1,000 attendees per session)
- ✅ **Webinar series** (recurring webinars)
- ✅ **Paid webinars** (charge for attendance)
- ✅ **Conversion funnels** (pre-built funnel templates)
- ✅ **Sales funnels** (full conversion flows)
- ✅ **Unlimited automations** (no step limits)

**For a course creator or coach running weekly webinars**:

- Separate Zoom Pro: $15/mo per host = ~$30/mo for 2 hosts
- Separate Leadpages or Landingi: $37-74/mo
- Total separate stack: **$67-104/mo**
- GetResponse Marketing Plus bundle: **$119/mo**
- Net savings: **up to $0-/$15/mo** — depending on usage

**Verdict on the bundle**: the webinar + landing page bundle pays back at scale, but the $119/mo entry point is steep for solopreneurs. For a creator doing 2+ webinars/month with paid attendance, the bundle genuinely saves $50+/mo vs separate tools.

## Marketing Automation ($159/mo) — the all-in

The Marketing Automation tier is for businesses that need the full feature set:

- ✅ Everything in Marketing Plus
- ✅ Visual automation builder (drag-and-drop canvas, no step limits)
- ✅ Lead scoring
- ✅ Advanced segmentation (behavioral, demographic, custom)
- ✅ Web push notifications
- ✅ Advanced reporting
- ✅ Priority support (faster chat response)

**Comparison vs ActiveCampaign Plus at $49/mo**:

- Both have visual automation builder
- ActiveCampaign has deeper conditional branching
- GetResponse has webinars bundled (ActiveCampaign doesn't)
- ActiveCampaign has native CRM (GetResponse doesn't)
- GetResponse at $159/mo is 3x more expensive than ActiveCampaign Plus

**For most businesses**, GetResponse's Marketing Automation tier at $159/mo is too expensive compared to ActiveCampaign Plus at $49/mo plus a separate webinar tool at $30-50/mo = **$79-99/mo total for similar capability**.

## Transactional email — another bundle option

Unlike ActiveCampaign or ConvertKit, **GetResponse does include transactional email on the Enterprise tier ($1,299+/mo)**. This is bundled with the most expensive tier only — for small businesses, transactional email still requires a separate service.

For high-volume transactional email use cases:
- **Brevo Lite** ($9/mo): transactional included on lowest tier — best bundle
- **MailerLite + MailerSend** ($20/mo total): mid-tier bundle
- **ActiveCampaign + Postmark** ($64/mo total): separate service stack
- **GetResponse transactional on Enterprise** ($1,299+/mo): only viable for 100K+ contacts

For businesses doing mixed marketing + transactional, **Brevo and MailerLite both beat GetResponse on pricing** unless you're already in GetResponse's enterprise pricing tier.

## Hidden costs nobody mentions at signup

Three charges that catch small businesses by surprise:

1. **Annual prepay discount only on first year**: GetResponse offers a 30% discount on annual prepay — but renewal reverts to standard pricing. Forgot to cancel before renewal? You'll pay full price for year 2.

2. **Webinar overage charges**: Marketing Plus includes 1,000 attendees per webinar. Larger webinar (>1,000 attendees) requires paid add-on: $79/mo for 5,000 attendees, $199/mo for 25,000. Not common for small businesses, but high-stakes webinars with 5K+ attendees can blow budget.

3. **Additional users/seats**: GetResponse doesn't charge per user by default, but Pro+ tiers with team features have a 5-seat limit. Beyond 5 seats, additional members cost $15/seat/month — similar to ActiveCampaign.

- **You're a course creator or coach doing weekly webinars** — bundle saves $50+/mo vs separate Zoom + Leadpages
- **You value landing pages included without separate Leadpages subscription** — saves $37-74/mo
- **You want a single EU-based vendor** — GetResponse is GDPR-compliant and EU-domiciled

- **You have < 500 subscribers** — MailerLite's free tier at 1,000 contacts is twice as generous
- **You don't need webinars or funnels** — Marketing Plus at $119/mo is 6x more expensive than necessary
- **You're an ecommerce store** — Klaviyo's Shopify integration and predictive analytics beat GetResponse for ecommerce
- **You're a creator selling courses** — Kit's commerce features are more relevant than GetResponse's webinars
- **You need deep conditional automations** — ActiveCampaign Plus at $49/mo has deeper branching than GetResponse's $159/mo Marketing Automation

## The verdict

GetResponse's pricing is **the right call for course creators and coaches doing weekly webinars** who can absorb the $119/mo Marketing Plus tier for the bundle value. For other use cases (small businesses under $100K, ecommerce, solopreneurs), GetResponse is **genuinely overpriced** at the bundle tiers.

The biggest "watch out": **the jump from $13/mo Starter to $119/mo Marketing Plus is 9x** — but most of the value lives in that $119/mo bundle. If you only need email + landing pages, GetResponse's Starter doesn't significantly beat MailerLite's $10/mo Starter, and you'd save by skipping the bundle.
