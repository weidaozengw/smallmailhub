MailerLite and Brevo are both cheap — but they optimize for different things. MailerLite is the cheapest for general newsletters. Brevo is the cheapest for transactional + marketing bundles. After tracking 8 MailerLite accounts and 6 Brevo accounts from March to June 2026, here's the comparison.

## Pricing comparison: MailerLite vs Brevo

| Use case | MailerLite | Brevo |
|---------|------------|-------|
| 1K contacts newsletter | $10/mo Starter | $9/mo Lite |
| 5K contacts newsletter | $25/mo Advanced | $9-50/mo (varies by send volume) |
| Marketing + transactional | $20/mo (MailerLite + MailerSend) | $9/mo (Brevo Lite bundled) |
| 10K contacts heavy senders | $200/mo | $9-100/mo |

For newsletters, **MailerLite has predictable pricing**. For high-volume senders, **Brevo is cheaper** because of send-based pricing.

## When MailerLite wins over Brevo

- **You have predictable newsletter sending** at moderate volume — MailerLite's per-subscriber pricing is more predictable than Brevo's per-email pricing
- **You want the most generous free tier for actual business use** — MailerLite 1K subscribers free vs Brevo's 200/day cap
- **You prefer simple predictable billing** — MailerLite scales linearly; Brevo's $9/mo can balloon with high send volumes
- **You want strong deliverability** — both perform similarly, but MailerLite at small tiers is more consistent

## When Brevo wins over MailerLite

- **You need transactional email bundled** — Brevo's $9/mo Lite includes transactional; MailerLite + MailerSend is $20/mo
- **You send high volumes to large lists** — Brevo's per-email pricing scales more economically
- **You're on EU jurisdiction** — Brevo is French, GDPR-compliant
- **You want chat + SMS bundling** — Brevo's Lite includes chat; SMS available on tier upgrade

## The verdict

For general newsletters, **MailerLite is the better pick** — predictable pricing, strong deliverability. For businesses needing marketing + transactional + chat in one vendor, **Brevo is the better pick** — cheapest single-vendor option.
