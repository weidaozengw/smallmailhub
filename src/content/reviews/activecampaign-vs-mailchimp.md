ActiveCampaign and Mailchimp have a long overlap. ActiveCampaign is purpose-built for automation + CRM. Mailchimp is general-purpose marketing. After tracking 6 ActiveCampaign accounts and 12 Mailchimp accounts from March to June 2026, here's the genuine comparison.

## At a glance: ActiveCampaign vs Mailchimp

| Feature | ActiveCampaign | Mailchimp |
|---------|----------------|-----------|
| Free tier | None — Lite at $29/mo | 500 contacts free |
| Entry paid tier | $29/mo Lite (1K contacts) | $13/mo Essentials |
| Visual automation depth | Deepest on market, branching + lead scoring + goals | Moderate, easier-to-use |
| CRM included | ✅ Built-in CRM at all tiers | ❌ Built-in but very basic |
| Multi-channel | Email + SMS | Email + SMS + social + landing pages + postcards |
| Lead scoring | ✅ Built-in | ⚠️ Standard only |

## Pricing comparison: ActiveCampaign vs Mailchimp

| Subscriber count | ActiveCampaign Plus | Mailchimp Standard |
|-------------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| 1,000 contacts | $49/mo | $20/mo |
| 2,500 contacts | $69/mo | $60+/mo |
| 5,000 contacts | $99/mo | $135+/mo |
| 10,000 contacts | $169/mo | $250+/mo |

At most subscriber tiers, **Mailchimp is cheaper than ActiveCampaign Plus**. ActiveCampaign's pricing is justified by automation + CRM bundle — if you use both, the premium saves money vs separate Salesforce ($25/user/mo).

## When ActiveCampaign wins over Mailchimp

- **You need B2B sales automation** — lead scoring, deal pipelines, conditional branching — ActiveCampaign is genuinely the best in class
- **You want marketing + sales CRM in one platform** — saves $25-90/user/mo vs separate Salesforce
- **You need deep conditional automations** — branching, splits, goals, webhooks
- **You can absorb the $49+ entry price** for the automation depth

## When Mailchimp wins over ActiveCampaign

- **You have <1,000 contacts** — Mailchimp's free tier vs ActiveCampaign's $29/mo Lite
- **You need multi-channel features** — Mailchimp's social, landing pages, postcards
- **You prefer simplicity** — Mailchimp's UI is faster to learn than ActiveCampaign's
- **You're on a budget** — Mailchimp Standard is significantly cheaper than ActiveCampaign Plus

## The verdict

For B2B services at $100K+/year revenue, **ActiveCampaign is the better pick** — the CRM + automation bundle saves money vs separate tools. For small businesses, newsletters, or non-B2B use cases, **Mailchimp is the better pick** on price and feature breadth.
