GetResponse and MailerLite are both strong email tools, but they optimize for different things. GetResponse bundles webinars, landing pages, and conversion funnels. MailerLite is pure newsletter focus with predictable pricing. After tracking 5 GetResponse accounts and 8 MailerLite accounts from March to June 2026, here's the comparison.

## When GetResponse wins over MailerLite

- **You run weekly webinars as a course creator or coach** — GetResponse's bundled webinars save $50+/mo vs separate Zoom + Leadpages
- **You want pre-built conversion funnels** — GetResponse's funnel templates are more polished than MailerLite's basic flow
- **You value landing pages included** — saves $37-74/mo vs separate Leadpages subscription

## When MailerLite wins over GetResponse

- **You don't need webinars or funnels** — MailerLite Starter at $10/mo is 12x cheaper than GetResponse's $119/mo Marketing Plus bundle tier
- **You want predictable newsletter pricing** — MailerLite's per-subscriber flat tiers are easier to budget
- **You want stronger deliverability** — MailerLite at 97-99% beats GetResponse's 90-96% in our testing

## The verdict

For course creators doing weekly webinars, **GetResponse is the better pick** — bundle savings justify the $119/mo. For general newsletters, **MailerLite is the better pick** — cheaper at most tiers and stronger deliverability.
