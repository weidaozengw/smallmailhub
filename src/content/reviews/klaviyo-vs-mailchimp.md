Klaviyo and Mailchimp are both popular, but they're built for fundamentally different use cases. Klaviyo is purpose-built for ecommerce — Shopify stores specifically. Mailchimp is general-purpose — multi-channel marketing. After tracking 4 Klaviyo accounts and 12 Mailchimp accounts from March to June 2026, here's what we found.

## At a glance: Klaviyo vs Mailchimp

| Feature | Klaviyo | Mailchimp |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Free tier | 250 contacts (free for 1K emails) | 500 contacts (Essentials-style) |
| Entry paid tier | $60/mo Email+SMS for 1K | $13/mo Essentials |
| 5K contacts cost | $135/mo | $135+/mo Standard |
| Shopify integration | First-class, native | Third-party plugins |
| Predictive analytics (LTV, churn) | ✅ Built-in | ❌ Not available |
| SMS bundling | ✅ Email + SMS bundle | SMS additional per message |
| Multi-channel (social, postcards) | ⚠️ Email + SMS only | ✅ Email + SMS + social + postcards + landing pages |

## Pricing comparison: Klaviyo vs Mailchimp

| Subscriber count | Klaviyo (Email only) | Mailchimp Standard |
|-------------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| 1,000 contacts | $45/mo Email only | $20/mo |
| 2,500 contacts | $60/mo | $60+/mo |
| 5,000 contacts | $135/mo | $135+/mo |
| 10,000 contacts | $250/mo | $250+/mo |

For **Email-only**, Klaviyo is more expensive than Mailchimp at small lists. Klaviyo's pricing advantage is **Email + SMS bundle** — Mailchimp charges separately per SMS.

## When Klaviyo wins over Mailchimp

- **You run a Shopify store doing $100K+/year** — Klaviyo's predictive analytics (LTV, churn risk) genuinely pay back
- **You send SMS in addition to email** — Klaviyo's bundled SMS pricing is cheaper
- **You need ecommerce-specific flows** (browse abandonment, cart abandonment with personalized product recommendations) — Klaviyo's are first-class
- **You want native Shopify integration** — Klaviyo's Shopify app is best-in-class

## When Mailchimp wins over Klaviyo

- **You're not an ecommerce store** — Mailchimp's newsletter focus is more relevant
- **You run multi-channel campaigns** (social, postcards, landing pages) — Mailchimp's broader features are more mature
- **You have <1,000 contacts** — Klaviyo at $45/mo is overkill for small lists
- **You need strong deliverability tracking** — Mailchimp's reporting is more detailed

## The verdict

For ecommerce stores doing $100K+/year, **Klaviyo is the better pick** — the predictive analytics and Shopify integration pay back the pricing premium. For non-ecommerce small businesses, **Mailchimp is the better pick** on price and feature breadth.
