Klaviyo and ActiveCampaign are both premium email tools, but they target fundamentally different use cases. Klaviyo is purpose-built for ecommerce (Shopify specifically). ActiveCampaign is purpose-built for B2B services. After tracking 4 Klaviyo accounts and 6 ActiveCampaign accounts from March to June 2026, here's the comparison.

## When Klaviyo wins over ActiveCampaign

- **You run a Shopify store doing $100K+/year** — Klaviyo's predictive analytics (LTV, churn risk) genuinely pay back
- **You want best-in-class ecommerce automations** — browse abandonment, cart abandonment, post-purchase flows
- **You send SMS + email** — Klaviyo's bundled SMS pricing is cheaper than ActiveCampaign's
- **You want native Shopify integration** — Klaviyo's app is best-in-class

## When ActiveCampaign wins over Klaviyo

- **You run a B2B service** (consulting, SaaS, agency) — ActiveCampaign's CRM + lead scoring pipeline is built for B2B sales
- **You want marketing + sales CRM in one platform** — saves $25-90/user/mo vs separate Salesforce
- **You need deep conditional automations** — branching, splits, goals for B2B nurture flows

## The verdict

For ecommerce stores, **Klaviyo is the better pick**. For B2B services, **ActiveCampaign is the better pick**. The two tools target different segments and shouldn't both be in your stack.
