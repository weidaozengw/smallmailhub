## The Ad Network

Beehiiv's **Ad Network** is the platform's headline feature. Here's how it works:

1. **Newsletter creators apply** to join the Ad Network (requires 1,000+ subscribers)
2. **Brands apply** to advertise via the Ad Network
3. **Beehiiv's algorithm matches** relevant brands with relevant newsletters
4. **Beehiiv handles sales, invoicing, and payment processing** — creators don't do outreach
5. **Creators earn** based on CPM (cost per mille) rates, typically $20-50 per 1,000 subscribers per ad placement

**Typical revenue:**
- 5,000-subscriber newsletter: $100-250 per sponsored placement
- 10,000-subscriber newsletter: $200-500 per placement
- 50,000-subscriber newsletter: $1,000-2,500 per placement

**For newsletters sending weekly:** Annual revenue from Ad Network can be $5,000-50,000+ depending on subscriber count and niche.

**What makes the Ad Network unique:**
- Creators don't do the sales work
- Beehiiv handles invoicing and late payments
- Ads are matched to relevant newsletters (better conversion for brands, better fit for readers)
- Creators can review and approve/decline each ad before it runs

**Substack doesn't have an equivalent feature.** Mailchimp and MailerLite don't either. The Ad Network is Beehiiv's competitive moat for newsletter monetization.

## Boosts referral program

Beehiiv's **Boosts** program is a unique referral mechanic:

1. Readers can refer new subscribers via unique referral links
2. When a referred subscriber signs up for a paid plan, the referrer gets a free subscription
3. This rewards engaged readers with paid access
4. Creates viral growth — readers actively promote the newsletter to earn paid access

**Substack has a similar referral program** but rewards are different (no paid access rewards).

**For paid newsletters:** Beehiiv's Boosts program creates a viral loop where engaged readers promote the newsletter to others. Substack's referral program is simpler but less viral.

**For free newsletters:** Both platforms have basic referral programs. The advantage is minimal.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Beehiiv better than Substack?

For monetization — yes, Beehiiv is significantly better (0% platform fee vs Substack's 10%, plus the Ad Network). For community and discovery — Substack is stronger. The right choice depends on your priorities.

### Does Beehiiv have automation?

No — Beehiiv has no automation workflows. For newsletters, this is fine. If you need automation (welcome series, course drips, abandoned cart), use MailerLite, Mailchimp, or ActiveCampaign.

### Can I monetize a free newsletter on Beehiiv?

Yes, via the Ad Network (requires 1,000+ subscribers and the Scale plan at $49/mo). Typical revenue: $20-50 CPM depending on niche.

### Does Beehiiv take a cut of paid subscription revenue?

No — Beehiiv takes 0% on the Scale plan. You keep 100% of paid subscription revenue, minus Stripe processing fees (~2.9% + 30¢ per transaction).

### Can I migrate from Substack to Beehiiv?

Yes — Beehiiv has a direct Substack importer. Subscribers and past posts transfer cleanly. Paid subscriptions require manual coordination with Stripe.

### Does Beehiiv have landing pages?

No — Beehiiv has newsletter signup forms but not standalone landing pages. If you need landing pages, use MailerLite or Carrd.

### Is Beehiiv's deliverability good?

Yes — Beehiiv uses reputable sending infrastructure and authentication. Inbox placement is 95-99% on Gmail, comparable to Substack and Mailchimp.
