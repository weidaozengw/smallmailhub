If you run an e-commerce store, you don't need the same email tool a creator or service business uses. You need one with deep Shopify/WooCommerce integration, revenue attribution, and product recommendation flows. This guide covers the six tools we'd actually recommend to e-commerce store owners in 2026.

## Quick answer: who's this guide for?

- You run a Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, or Squarespace store
- You want email + SMS + transactional in one tool
- You have 500-50,000 customers in your email list
- You do at least $50K/year in revenue

If any of those don't describe you, you might be better off with our general [best email marketing tools](/best/best-email-marketing-tools-for-small-business/) guide.

## How we picked these six

We started with the 30+ email marketing platforms marketed to e-commerce, then filtered to these six based on:

1. **Direct Shopify/WooCommerce integration** (not just Zapier)
2. **Revenue attribution per email** (which emails drove sales)
3. **Product recommendation blocks** (dynamic content based on browse history)
4. **SMS marketing capability** (e-commerce needs email + SMS together)
5. **Transactional email option** (order confirmations, shipping updates)

The remaining six above are what survived.

## The 6 best email marketing tools for e-commerce in 2026

### 1. Klaviyo — Best overall for e-commerce doing $500K+/year

**Pricing:** Free up to 250 contacts · $45/mo Email · $60/mo Email + SMS

Klaviyo was built from day one for e-commerce. Its Shopify integration is the deepest in the industry — every order, browse event, and customer action syncs in real-time, enabling hyper-personalized email and SMS flows.

**Best for:** Stores doing $500K+/year that need predictive analytics and revenue attribution

**Skip if:** Your store does under $100K/year — Klaviyo is overkill until you need its predictive features

### 2. Brevo — Best for small e-commerce doing under $500K/year

**Pricing:** Free up to 300 emails/day · $9/mo Starter · $18/mo Standard

Brevo bundles marketing + transactional + SMS on every plan. For small e-commerce stores, this replaces 2-3 separate tools (Mailchimp + SendGrid + Postscript) at a fraction of the cost.

**Best for:** Stores doing $50K-$500K/year that need transactional email and SMS without paying for three separate tools

**Skip if:** Your store does $1M+/year — Klaviyo's predictive features are worth the premium at that scale

### 3. Mailchimp — Best for multi-channel e-commerce marketing

**Pricing:** Free up to 500 contacts · $20/mo Essentials · $60/mo Standard

Mailchimp's e-commerce features have matured significantly. The Shopify/WooCommerce integration includes product blocks, abandoned cart flows, and revenue attribution. If you also run ads or social media marketing, Mailchimp bundles everything.

**Best for:** Stores that need email + ads + social + brand management in one platform

**Skip if:** Your store is purely transactional email + marketing email (Brevo is cheaper)

### 4. ActiveCampaign — Best for e-commerce with complex sales cycles

**Pricing:** $29/mo Lite · $49/mo Plus · $89/mo Professional

ActiveCampaign's automation builder is more powerful than Mailchimp's, Brevo's, or MailerLite's. For e-commerce stores with complex sales cycles (high-ticket items, B2B e-commerce, custom products), ActiveCampaign's conditional logic and lead scoring unlock flows the others can't.

**Best for:** High-ticket e-commerce, B2B e-commerce, stores with custom product configurations

**Skip if:** Your store sells low-cost, simple products with standard checkout flows

### 5. MailerLite — Best budget option for new e-commerce stores

**Pricing:** Free up to 1,000 subscribers · $10/mo for 1,000 subscribers

MailerLite is the cheapest serious option for new e-commerce stores. The Shopify/WooCommerce integration is basic but functional, covering the essential flows: welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase, win-back.

**Best for:** New stores doing under $50K/year that need email marketing on a tight budget

**Skip if:** You need predictive analytics, revenue attribution, or deep personalization

### 6. Sendlane — Best for e-commerce doing $1M+/year

**Pricing:** $29/mo (1,000 contacts) · Custom for higher tiers

Sendlane is the lesser-known competitor to Klaviyo for serious e-commerce stores. It includes deep Shopify integration, behavior-based automation, and a unified email + SMS + customer data platform.

**Best for:** Stores doing $1M+/year that want Klaviyo-style features with better support

**Skip if:** Your store is under $500K/year — Sendlane's premium features aren't justified at smaller scale

## How to choose: a 4-question decision tree

**Q1: What's your annual revenue?**
- Under $50K → MailerLite
- $50K-$500K → Brevo
- $500K-$1M → Klaviyo
- $1M+ → Sendlane or Klaviyo (both are strong)

**Q2: Do you also need transactional email?**
- Yes → Brevo (bundled) or Klaviyo (add-on)
- No → Mailchimp or MailerLite

**Q3: Do you have a complex sales cycle?**
- Yes (B2B, custom products, high-ticket) → ActiveCampaign
- No → Klaviyo or Brevo

**Q4: Do you need SMS marketing?**
- Yes (US, UK, EU, AU, BR) → Klaviyo, Brevo, or Sendlane (all include SMS)
- No → Mailchimp or MailerLite

## What we don't recommend

These platforms get mentioned a lot in e-commerce email marketing lists, but we don't recommend them:

- **Constant Contact** — interface feels dated, e-commerce integration is shallow
- **SendGrid** — built for transactional only, not marketing email
- **Mailjet** — feature gap with the six above at the same price
- **HubSpot Marketing Hub** — way too expensive for small e-commerce ($800/mo starting price)
- **Salesforce Marketing Cloud** — enterprise pricing, no fit for stores under $5M/year

## Bottom line

For most e-commerce store owners reading this, **Brevo is the right answer at small scale ($50K-$500K revenue), Klaviyo at mid-scale ($500K-$1M), and either Klaviyo or Sendlane at large scale ($1M+).**

Start with Brevo if you're just getting started with email marketing for your store. Move to Klaviyo once Brevo's automation depth becomes a bottleneck. Move to Sendlane when Klaviyo's pricing becomes painful at scale.

## Frequently asked questions

### Which email tool has the best Shopify integration?

Klaviyo. It was built for Shopify from day one and has the deepest integration in the industry.

### Do I need a separate transactional email tool?

Not if you use Brevo, Klaviyo, or Sendlane — all three include transactional email. Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, and MailerLite require separate tools (SendGrid, Postmark) for transactional.

### Which tool has the best abandoned cart recovery?

Klaviyo (most sophisticated flows), Brevo (good basics, cheaper), Mailchimp (standard flows, mid-pricing).

### Is Klaviyo worth the high pricing?

For stores doing $500K+/year, yes. The predictive analytics and revenue attribution pay for themselves. For smaller stores, Brevo or MailerLite is enough.
