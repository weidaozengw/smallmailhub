### Mailchimp pricing 2026

Mailchimp remains the most recognized brand but its pricing jumps hard at 2,500 contacts ($13/mo Essentials to $60+/mo Premiere custom). For businesses that use Mailchimp's bundled tools (social posting, brand kit, landing pages), the premium pays back. For businesses that only want email, Mailchimp is overpriced compared to MailerLite or Brevo.

### Brevo pricing 2026

Brevo's flat $9/mo + overage pricing model is the cleanest for high-volume senders to large lists. For businesses with 5,000+ contacts sending 20K+ emails/month, Brevo can be 30-50% cheaper than Mailchimp or MailerLite. Brevo's transactional email bundling is the standout feature — MailerLite and Mailchimp charge separately.

### Klaviyo pricing 2026

Klaviyo's linear $1 per 250 contacts pricing model is the cleanest for ecommerce stores growing predictably. For Shopify stores doing $500K+/year, Klaviyo is the no-brainer choice — predictive analytics, browse abandonment, and SMS bundling earn the premium. For non-ecommerce stores, Klaviyo's toolset overweights product-specific features.

### ConvertKit (Kit) pricing 2026

Kit is the creator-focused email marketing platform, now free up to 10,000 subscribers with basic automation. Creator tier at $9/mo for the first 1,000 subscribers scales linearly to $415/mo at 50K. Kit's pricing sweet spot is the 3,000-25,000 creator range where commerce features and the creator-network sponsorship marketplace pay back the premium over MailerLite. The biggest gap: Kit does not include transactional email in any tier, so creators selling courses or paid subscriptions pay $15-20/mo extra for a separate transactional service.

### ActiveCampaign pricing 2026

ActiveCampaign is the deepest automation platform, but also one of the most expensive entry points. Lite at $29/mo lacks the visual automation canvas that defines it; Plus at $49/mo is where ActiveCampaign becomes competitive. ActiveCampaign's pricing is the right call for B2B service businesses at $100K+/year revenue (consulting, agencies, SaaS) that need CRM + automation in one platform. For ecommerce or creators, ActiveCampaign is overpriced compared to Klaviyo or Kit.

### GetResponse pricing 2026

GetResponse is the all-in-one marketing platform — bundling email, landing pages, webinars, and conversion funnels in one product. The Marketing Plus tier at $119/mo is where the bundle value kicks in (webinars + funnels), but Starter at $13/mo doesn't significantly beat MailerLite's $10/mo. GetResponse's pricing is the right call for course creators and coaches doing weekly webinars who can absorb $119/mo for the bundle value. For most small businesses, the $119/mo entry to the bundle is too steep.

## The hidden costs nobody mentions at signup

Three categories of hidden cost that often surprise new users:

### 1. Tier upgrade trap

Most tools charge progressively higher tier prices as you pass specific contact thresholds:
- Mailchimp: 500 contacts → 2,500 contacts (Essentials $13→$20→$60+)
- ActiveCampaign: 500 contacts → 1,000 contacts → 5,000 contacts
- MailerLite (less brutal): 1,000 → 2,500 → 5,000 contacts

If your business adds 50 contacts per month and you started at 480, you'll hit a tier jump at 500 that more than doubles your bill.

### 2. Transactional email add-ons

- **Mailchimp + Mandrill**: $13/mo Mailchimp Essentials + $20/mo Mandrill base = $33/mo minimum, plus per-email fees
- **MailerLite + MailerSend**: $10/mo MailerLite Starter + $10/mo MailerSend = $20/mo minimum
- **Brevo**: $9/mo Lite with transactional included = $9/mo total
- **Klaviyo Email + SMS**: $60/mo with bundled SMS (covers transactional too) = $60/mo total

The cost difference between Mailchimp's expensive setup and Brevo's all-in-one approach is $24/mo for the same deliverability and features.

### 3. SMS pricing volatility

For ecommerce stores that want SMS:
- Klaviyo bundles SMS at $60/mo (5K contacts) with 1,000 included
- ActiveCampaign, MailerLite, Mailchimp require separate SMS via Twilio or their gateway ($20-50/mo extra)
- Brevo doesn't include SMS in Lite, but their SMS add-on is $20/mo

## FAQ

### Which email marketing tool is cheapest for a small business under 1,000 contacts?

MailerLite. The free tier covers 1,000 subscribers at $0, and Starter at $10/mo is the cheapest comprehensive paid plan for businesses under 1,000 contacts. Compare to Mailchimp's free tier at 250 contacts and Brevo's 300-email-per-day cap.

### When does Mailchimp stop being the cheapest option?

2,500 contacts is the inflection point. Below 2,500, Mailchimp's tiered pricing stays under $20/mo for many businesses. Above 2,500, Mailchimp moves to "Premiere custom pricing" which starts at $60+/mo and scales aggressively. For businesses passing 2,500 contacts, switching to MailerLite, Brevo, or Klaviyo saves $40-200+/mo.

### What's the cheapest email marketing tool that includes transactional email?

Brevo Lite at $9/mo bundles marketing email, transactional email, SMS, and a basic CRM. By comparison:
- Mailchimp Essentials + Mandrill = $33/mo minimum
- MailerLite Starter + MailerSend = $20/mo minimum
- ActiveCampaign Lite + separate Twilio = $49+/mo minimum

For ecommerce stores doing transactional + marketing email from one platform, Brevo saves $20+/mo compared to the alternatives.

### What's the best email marketing tool for an ecommerce store on Shopify?

Klaviyo, hands down. The Shopify integration is native, predictive analytics are unique, and SMS bundling is included. For stores doing $500K+/year, Klaviyo's premium ($180-300/mo) pays back via better abandoned cart and browse abandonment automation.

For smaller Shopify stores doing <$100K/year, Brevo or MailerLite is more economical.

### Do annual prepay plans save money?

Yes, but the savings vary:
- MailerLite: 15% off annual
- Brevo: 20% off annual (most generous)
- Mailchimp: 15% off annual
- ActiveCampaign: 17% off annual
- Klaviyo: ~15% effective (but pricing is already per-contact)

If you're confident you'll stick with a tool for 12 months, annual prepay saves $50-200/year.
