# Affiliate Program Application Checklist

For MailPilot (smallmailhub.com). All 7 email tools reviewed need affiliate links before the first commission can be earned.

## Application priority order (by approval likelihood + commission size)

### Tier 1 — Apply immediately after domain is live

#### 1. MailerLite Partner Program (DIRECT, no middleman)
- **Commission:** 30% recurring for 12 months on paid plans
- **Cookie window:** 60 days
- **Approval time:** Instant (auto-approved after email verification)
- **Requirements:**
  - Valid email
  - Website URL (smallmailhub.com)
  - Tax info (W-8BEN for non-US)
- **Why first:** Instant approval, highest recurring %, no middleman fees
- **Apply:** https://www.mailerlite.com/affiliates

#### 2. Amazon Associates
- **Commission:** 1-4% on digital products (low for our niche but it's Amazon)
- **Cookie window:** 24 hours
- **Approval time:** 1-3 days
- **Requirements:**
  - Website with at least 10-15 published posts (we'll have this after day 1)
  - Account in good standing
  - W-8BEN if non-US
- **Why second:** Trusted by users, easy approval, links to books/courses about email marketing
- **Apply:** https://affiliate-program.amazon.com

### Tier 2 — Apply after 10-20 published posts (need traffic proof)

#### 3. ShareASale (network — hosts Brevo, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, AWeber, others)
- **Commission varies by advertiser:**
  - Brevo: 100% first month (one-time) or $5 per signup
  - ConvertKit: 30% recurring for 12 months
  - ActiveCampaign: 30% recurring for 12 months
  - AWeber: 30% recurring for 12 months
- **Cookie window:** 30-60 days (varies by advertiser)
- **Approval time:** 3-7 days for ShareASale network, then 1-3 days per advertiser
- **Requirements:**
  - Website with 50+ monthly visitors (use Cloudflare Analytics + GA4)
  - Original content (we have this)
  - No coupon/rebate content
- **Why third:** Highest concentration of email marketing advertisers
- **Apply:** https://account.shareasale.com/apply

#### 4. PartnerStack (network — hosts Kit/ConvertKit, Brevo, others)
- **Commission varies:**
  - Kit (ConvertKit): 30% recurring for 24 months (better than ShareASale)
  - Brevo: 100% first month + $5 per signup
- **Cookie window:** 60-90 days
- **Approval time:** 5-14 days
- **Requirements:**
  - Active website with editorial content
  - Audience relevance (we have this)
- **Why fourth:** Better Kit commission than ShareASale
- **Apply:** https://www.partnerstack.com/affiliate-programs

#### 5. Impact (network — hosts GetResponse, AWeber, others)
- **Commission varies:**
  - GetResponse: 33% recurring for 12 months
  - AWeber: 30% recurring for 12 months
- **Cookie window:** 30-60 days
- **Approval time:** 3-7 days
- **Requirements:**
  - Active website
  - US tax form or W-8BEN
- **Apply:** https://impact.com

### Tier 3 — Apply after 30+ published posts + 1,000+ monthly visitors

#### 6. CJ Affiliate (network)
- More selective than ShareASale, but hosts higher-paying advertisers
- Apply only after we have proven traffic

#### 7. Individual vendor programs (Brevo Direct, ActiveCampaign Direct, etc.)
- Some vendors have their own programs outside networks
- Typically harder to get into than networks
- Apply after we have brand recognition

## Tax setup

### W-8BEN form (required for ALL affiliate programs as non-US resident)
- **Purpose:** Claim treaty benefits, avoid 30% withholding on US-source income
- **Form location:** Available inside each affiliate program dashboard
- **Required info:**
  - Full legal name (Chinese passport name in pinyin)
  - Permanent address (China address)
  - Tax identification number (Chinese ID number or passport number)
  - Country of citizenship: China
  - Treat country: US
  - Article reference: Article 2 of US-China tax treaty (royalties/articles)
  - Signature + date
- **Format:** PDF form, sign digitally or print+scan

### Payoneer setup
- Already configured for KDP
- Same account works for all affiliate program payouts
- Most programs pay via Payoneer, ACH, or check
- Payoneer is preferred — fastest, lowest fees

## Affiliate link insertion strategy

For each review/alternatives page, place affiliate links at 3 conversion points:
1. **First mention** (after intro paragraph) — soft CTA
2. **Pricing section** (inline with price table) — hard CTA
3. **Final recommendation** (bottom of page) — closing CTA

Use `rel="sponsored noopener"` attribute (Google requirement + best practice).
